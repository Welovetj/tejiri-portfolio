A Developer

I'm Tejiri Efemini — a Nigerian developer based in Calgary, Alberta, currently studying at Mount Royal University.

I build clean, purposeful software across the full stack — from server-side PHP and dynamic Next.js apps to polished front-end experiences. Every line of code is written with intention and craft.

Outside the screen I'm in the pool, in the gym, or deep in a game. Driven by curiosity, shaped by culture, always shipping something new.
