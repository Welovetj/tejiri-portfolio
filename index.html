About

A portfolio with a strong first impression

I'm Tejiri Efemini, a student developer based in Calgary with a focus on building interfaces that feel sharp, modern, and alive.

I work across front-end and full-stack projects, balancing visual quality with solid structure. The goal is simple: make useful things and make them feel good to use.
